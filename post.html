Family

My Upbringing

Growing up I was given a unique perspective of life. My parents and I moved several times allowing me to experience different parts of the United States

I was born in Boise, Idaho. I lived here for 12 years of my life. My time was spent with my three siblings, two older sisters and a younger brother. My mom was a stay-at-home mom and planned exciting activities for us.

When I was 12 we picked up and moved to Tucson, Arizona, a fairly large city just 80 miles north of the border. Here I completed middle school. While middle school is tough on its own, moving in the middle of it also added to the difficulty.

At 14, just as I entered high school, we picked up and moved to New Hampshire. This was my favorite move of them all. I went through my pivital years here and am so grateful for the time spent.
